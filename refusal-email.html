Writing an Email — Refusing Politely

In this task, you will learn how to refuse politely in French while keeping a friendly tone.

Structure

All good responses follow this structure:

  1. Greeting
  2. Thanking
  3. Positive reaction
  4. Refusal
  5. Reason
  6. Apology
  7. Alternative suggestion
  8. Closing
